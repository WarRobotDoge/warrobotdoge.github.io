About

BookWorm for Tag TV is certainly a rare find. According to the only source about this game, it was coded in C++, and was the #4 game for TAG TV. The indie layout of this game reminds me of BookWorm for the Xbox. The game's graphics use no real-time blending or compositing, except for the fonts. BookWorm for TAG was actually multiplayer! I'm not exactly sure how it worked, or what "pass the remote", and "house to house" were, but I assume "pass the remote" was local. Seeing that TAG (Thunder), was "cloud-based", perhaps "house to house" was online multiplayer, though I am not sure. If you know anything about this version of BookWorm, please let me know! BookWorm was also on a TV game channel for DirecTV, it is now considered lost media, and there is no footage of this version.
