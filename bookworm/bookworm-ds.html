A S-P-A-S-S-O with fun with Bookworm for DS!

Give free rein to your creative spirit with this downloadable version of PopCap's successful puzzle lyricist, now in single mode for DS! Connect the tiles left, right, top and bottom to form words and feed Lex in the Classic Bookworm mode. Be careful though, to the fiery letters: they could be disastrous for you and Lex!

Use prize cards, compose bonus words to increase your score and connect the best words to enter the Hall of Fame. Have fun and learn new words with this puzzle lyricist with limitless playability. Refine your mind and relax with every game!
